Reimagining E-Waste as India’s Next Mineral Reserve

Welcome to RareVerse — where discarded electronics transform into a new source of national strength. We’re pioneering a low-cost, eco-conscious technology to extract rare earth elements (REEs) and precious metals from e-waste, powered by a smart AI platform that tracks every gram from recovery to resale. With a recovery rate of over 90%, 85% lower environmental footprint, and full digital traceability, RareVerse is more than a tagline: Mining Tomorrow from Yesterday
